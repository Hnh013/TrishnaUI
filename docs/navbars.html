Navbars

The navigation bar or simply navbar is one of the most important parts of a website. Usually at the top, it tells users about the information and actions relating to the current screen, with the mostly used actions present in form of links.

Simple Navbars

Navbars can be like a simple navbar with brand name on one side , and some important links on the other. Usually they do not change their shape or orientation to a great extent, upon changing the viewport size.
