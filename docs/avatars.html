Avatars

Avatars are images representing a user or member of a group. In websites users upload an image to represent themselves. The avatar can be a real picture of a person using it, or something relatable to him/her , or an alter ego of the person.

Round Avatars

These avatars are graphics and pictures, in round or circular shape, to represent the user identity.

Rounded Square Avatars

These avatars are graphics and pictures, in square shape with round edges, to represent the user identity.

Square Avatars

These avatars are graphics and pictures, in square shape, to represent the user identity.
