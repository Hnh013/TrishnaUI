Alerts

These are used to notify the user of an event that requires his/her attention triggering the alert, or something that reuires an action by the users, which may be immediate or not depending upon what triggered the alert in the first place.

Usually displayed on top or bottom of the screens, they are used for the purpose of capturing the user's attention

Alerts are colored or themed based on what is the urgency of action or event, like yellow and similar colors to warn users of a potential harmful action, red while performing an irreversible action , green for successful completion of a transaction,etc.
