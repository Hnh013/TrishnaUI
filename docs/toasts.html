Snackbars/Toasts

Usually placed at bottom of the screen, snackbars and toasts provide an immediate notification, that may or may not require an action on the user's end.

Snackbars

Snackbars usually contain a single line of text that tells a feedback of a performed action and may or may not contain any action, other than the one to dismiss the snackbar itself
