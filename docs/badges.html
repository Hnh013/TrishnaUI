Badges

Badges are small pictorial representation that gives a small information about a specific attribute of the entity it is attached to. Badges can be used for representing a user is online or not , or telling number of notifications you have.

Blip Badges

These blip badges are single colored badges, that can be used to represent a specific stage of the entity.

Ring Badges

These ring badges are single colored badges, that can be used to represent a specific stage of the entity, like status, or availability.

Ring Badges (with Effects)

Moon Ring Badges

These are special badges that can be attributed to a specific status of night or night related , or offline or idle

Mobile Badges

These are special badges that can be added to give a specific attribute related to mobile or availability.

Numbered Blip Badges

Similar to blip badges , but can show a quantity associated with the entity. The quantity can be number of messages, emails, etc. from the entity
