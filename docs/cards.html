Cards

Cards are a small rectanglular entities associated to a single aspect. Cards can contain elements such as text, links, buttons or images but their main purpose is to convince the ser to click upon them to view more details.

Image Cards

The are the cards that contain only an image, usually done for advertizing purposes, inducing a curiosity in the mind of the observer
