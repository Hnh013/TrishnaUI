Installation Guide to TrishnaUI

TrishnaUI is a component library made primarily in HTML and CSS, this is done in order to maintain the simplicity for the developers while designing components with the help of this library. Also not just making components, developers have the option of customizing and adding small tweaks to their components via a large array of utiliy classes of CSS properties so that they can make the project of their dreams, well at least they can reach the closest intentions.

Usage

To use TrishnaUI in your project, just copy the link given below and put it in the HEAD tag of your HTML file, the library will take care of the rest. Best of Luck !
