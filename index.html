Build Fast , Build Scalable , Build Responsive , Build as you wish with TrishnaUI

TrishnaUI helps you to design all device friendly and responsive websites with minimum effort , with less time spent on learning , you can build scalable and highly customizable websites for your project.

It's Responsive

Works fine on device & orientation
We have tried and tested each one of our components so that given the devide viewport the component adjusts the best possible way to fit in . Also we utilize a simple breakpoint system in case you want to have an idea of how we set the values according to viewport changes.

It's Fast

Get started quickly
To get started you do not need to downlaod anything , you just need to put in the link of the CDN in your stylesheet url and you're done. Now you can choose whatever components you want and wherever you want to style with our library, that all with just a link.

It's what you want

Want to play & Customize ? Come along
You can choose our styles and colors and font families if you want , or even better , you can choose your favorites and can customize the library components according to your needs. For this we provide lots of utility classes and options to give custom values to attributes
